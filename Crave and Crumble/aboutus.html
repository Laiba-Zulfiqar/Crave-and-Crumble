About Crave & Crumble

Founded in Lahore in 2023, the philosophy of Crave And Crumble Bakeshop was simple; to create connections through desserts while striking the ideal balance between flavor and feeling.


Since the beginning, Crave And Crumble has secured an irreplaceable spot in the hearts of people.


Being the quintessential representation of aesthetics and taste, a Layers dessert is consumed by the eyes well before delighting the tastebuds!

Reaching Nationwide

Growing And Expanding

Indulge your senses and embark on a delectable journey at Crave and Crumble, where we are dedicated to crafting the most exquisite desserts in town. With a passion for perfection and a love for layers, our desserts are guaranteed to delight your taste buds and transport you to a sweet utopia.


What started as a small bakery in Lahore has now evolved into a dessert empire with eight stores in various locations, including Lahore and Islamabad. We are humbled by the overwhelming love and support we have received from our dessert enthusiasts, and this is only the beginning of our sweet adventure
